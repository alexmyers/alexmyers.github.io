RAWerTUNES10.EXE (3Dude Remix)

RAWerTUNES10.EXE is a noise-art album made in homage to the now late, (great?) iTunes 10 release.

As iTunes 11 makes its way onto computers across the globe this album will remain as a media-archive of splendid noises transcoded from iTunes 10 executables. Exapted from the same raw code each track is now accompanied by its own 'Dude' ready to be 3D printed, at your own (great) expense.

made in collaboration with Daniel Rourke for Run, Computer Run at Rua Red, Dublin, Ireland.
