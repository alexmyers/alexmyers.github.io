Writing Things We Can No Longer Read

Writing Things We Can No Longer Read is a surrealist artgame about the beauty of apophenia. It is the fourth iteration in a series of ephemeral artgames that address the human need to find meaning.

We transcribe significance constantly in our daily lives. This writing increases until it reaches a critical mass of meaninglessness and then eventually it finds its way into the realm of meaning again.

I hope.

Developed, in part, for a 12 hour game jam, updated for a gallery exhibition, then later a conference and continually evolving.
